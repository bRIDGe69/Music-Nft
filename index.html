About Me

I am a solo artist offering unique music tracks as NFTs. You can mint my music for a small fee and own a piece of my creative work. Each track is a one-of-a-kind NFT that gives you exclusive rights to my music.

Minting Agreements

  • Exclusive Rights: The buyer owns the music track exclusively. Limited to 1 NFT per track.
  • Limited Edition: Limited number of NFTs available for each track.
  • Regular License: Unlimited NFTs available for each track with standard usage rights.
